# Dr. Bartender — Class Menu & Pricing

## Overview

All classes are **$35/person** with an **8 guest minimum** and **20 guest maximum**. Each class is **2 hours** and includes digital recipe cards and class materials accessible via QR code. One instructor handles all class sizes.

Supply add-ons are available for clients who want Dr. Bartender to provide all spirits, mixers, garnishes, and materials. BYOB clients provide their own alcohol; Dr. Bartender brings the expertise and equipment.

---

## 1. Mixology 101

Learn the fundamentals of cocktail making with 2-3 classic cocktails and hands-on technique training.

**What's covered:**
- 2-3 classic cocktails (Old Fashioned, Margarita, Martini rotation)
- Basic techniques: shaking, stirring, muddling, proper measuring, ice handling
- Digital materials via QR code

**Supply add-on:** $25/person
- Spirits: Tito's Vodka, Jim Beam Bourbon, Bacardi Rum, 1800 Tequila, Bombay Gin
- All mixers, garnishes, ice, and disposables included

## 3. Margarita Workshop

A deep dive into the margarita — exploring how different recipes and ingredient choices change the drink.

**What's covered:**
- 3-4 margarita variations exploring different base recipes
- Recipe education: triple sec/Cointreau vs OJ & simple syrup vs agave syrup vs Grand Marnier
- How each ingredient choice changes the drink
- Flavor additions: spicy, mango, strawberry, etc.
- Digital materials via QR code

**Supply add-on:** $25/person
- Tequila, all recipe variations, fresh fruit, mixers, garnishes, ice, and disposables included

---

## 4. Tropical / Tiki Night

A fun, colorful class exploring the world of tiki cocktails and rum-based drinks.

**What's covered:**
- 4 cocktails: Mai Tai, Piña Colada, Rum Punch, Painkiller
- Education on rum styles, tropical techniques, tiki culture
- Fun extras: falernum, orgeat, cinnamon syrup
- Digital materials via QR code

**Supply add-on:** $30/person
- Multiple rum styles (white, dark, spiced), coconut cream, pineapple, orgeat, falernum, cinnamon syrup, garnishes, ice, and disposables included

---

## 5. Brunch Cocktails

Master the art of brunch drinks — from building a proper Bloody Mary to shaking an Espresso Martini.

**What's covered:**
- 4 cocktails: Mimosa variations, Bloody Mary build, Espresso Martini, Aperol Spritz
- Education on champagne cocktails, building a Bloody Mary bar, espresso techniques
- Digital materials via QR code

**Supply add-on:** $30/person
- Prosecco/champagne, vodka, Aperol, espresso liqueur, juices, Bloody Mary fixings, garnishes, ice, and disposables included

---

## 6. Mocktail Workshop

Virgin versions of popular cocktails — same techniques, same flavor, no alcohol. Great for corporate events, inclusive gatherings, and anyone who wants to know what to order at the bar.

**What's covered:**
- 4 virgin cocktails: Mojito, Espresso Martini, Margarita, Piña Colada
- Education on building flavor without alcohol using the same techniques as the real thing
- Digital materials via QR code

**Supply add-on:** $15/person
- All juices, syrups, sodas, garnishes, ice, and disposables included
