# Staff ops backlog, captured 2026-07-22

Raw decisions and rules from Dallas during the event-details brainstorm. This file is the source of truth for the specs that follow Project A (docs/superpowers/specs/2026-07-22-staff-event-details-design.md). Nothing here is built yet.

## Project B: logistics duties + duty pay (next up)

Assignment:

- Equipment and supply responsibilities are assignable to a specific staff member or members on multi-staff events. Sources vary per event: storage-unit pickup, grocery pickup or delivery order, or both.
- Duties surface on that staffer's event details page.

Pay rules (money seam, read .claude/seam-sweep-2026-07-02.md before planning):

- Auto-add duty pay to payroll from the booking instead of admin memory: if the client requested/paid for a thing (menu print, parking, equipment add-ons), the matching staff pay line is created automatically. Removable at pay time.
- Run-payroll attribution popup: for multi-staff events, clicking Run payroll asks who gets paid for each booked duty (who printed the menu, who brought the bar, who did the supply order). Single-staff events auto-attribute.
- Equipment and Supplies fee: flat $20. Trigger: the client PAID at least $50 for storage-pickup add-ons on the booking (bar rental, bubble gun, glassware, carbonation kit, and the like). ONE fee covering all pickup duties for the event, not per add-on.
- Bar rental share: bringing your own bar OR using a DRB bar (pickup and dropoff at the Pilsen storage unit) qualifies for the same $20.
- Hosted events pay HOURLY for equipment and supply handling, never the $20 fee. Policy numbers: 90 minutes setup, up to 2.5 hours supply handling.
- Menu print: flat $5. Frame required; frames to be stocked at Pilsen storage; menu + frame are left with the client. Tablet/iPad display is an acceptable alternative (clean, on a stand, decent size). Planning size for framed menus is 8x10.

Bar kit policy (copy ships in Project A, restated here for completeness):

- A small handled cooler is part of the standard bar kit (about 3 cases of beer plus ice, or 2x20lb ice bags with the lid ajar; bar mats, ice bins, tip jar ride inside it). We bring one even when the client has coolers. Reference point: that cooler supports chill, rotate, and serve for beer and wine at around 100 guests.

## Project C: receipt reimbursements

- Staff submit a receipt image plus a chosen amount. Receipt must be itemized. Submit goes to admin for approve or deny. Approved amounts land in the staffer's payout.
- Fraud edges to design for: duplicate receipt submissions, amount vs receipt mismatch.
